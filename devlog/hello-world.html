Hello, welcome to the devlog

I've spent years shipping software, but most of what I learn never makes it past my own notebooks. This is where that changes, a place to think out loud about building accessible, privacy-first apps, and the research that sits underneath them.

What you'll find here

Short notes, mostly. Why I made a particular design choice. Something a user taught me that I hadn't seen coming. Where the tinnitus research stands. The occasional rabbit hole in on-device AI. Some posts will be technical; others will just be honest.

Design for one, help everyone.

Why write it down

Because the best parts of these apps came from listening, to my husband, to dyslexic readers, to people who'd felt unseen. Writing keeps me listening. And maybe a note here helps someone building something of their own.

More soon.

— Nat
