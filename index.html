About Me

I'm an iOS developer with production-scale experience shipping apps to millions of users. I'm currently pursuing a Master's in Artificial Intelligence while building personal passion projects: an adaptive audio masking app for tinnitus and a dyslexia-friendly reading app.

Accessibility is the thread that runs through everything I build. My mission is the curb cut effect — a term I first heard in a talk by Chris Ward: ramps cut into curbs for wheelchairs ended up helping everyone — strollers, suitcases, delivery carts. Software built for people with tinnitus or dyslexia ends up smoothing the path for every listener and every reader. So I cut curbs.
